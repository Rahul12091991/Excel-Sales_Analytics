## Sales Report :


- **Project objective:** 

    **1.** Create a _[customer performance report](https://github.com/KirandeepMarala/Excel-Sales_Analysis/blob/main/Customer%20Performance%20Report.pdf)_ 

    **2.** Conduct a comprehensive comparison between _[market performance and sales targets](https://github.com/KirandeepMarala/Excel-Sales_Analysis/blob/main/Customer%20Performance%20Report.pdf)_

- **Purpose of sales analytics:** Leveraging data insights for optimizing sales performance, forecasting trends, and enhancing customer relationships.

- **Importance of analyzing sales data:** Understanding market trends, identifying growth opportunities, and making informed business decisions.

- **Role of reports:** Determine effective customer discounts, facilitate negotiations with consumers, and identify potential business expansion opportunities in promising countries.


## Finance Report :

- **Project objective:** 

    **1.** Create Profit and Loss (P&L) reports by _[Fiscal Year](https://github.com/KirandeepMarala/Excel-Sales_Analysis/blob/main/P%26L%20Statement%20by%20Fiscal%20Year.pdf)_ & _[Months](https://github.com/KirandeepMarala/Excel-Sales_Analysis/blob/main/P%26L%20Statement%20by%20Months.pdf)_

- **Purpose of finance analytics:** Utilizing data-driven insights for enhancing financial performance, mitigating risks, and supporting strategic decision-making within organizations.

- **Importance of analyzing Finance data:** Understanding the financial health of an organization, identifying trends, detecting anomalies, and making informed decisions to drive profitability, manage risks, and achieve long-term financial sustainability.

- **Role of reports:** Align financial planning with strategic goals Instill confidence in the organization's financial outlook.
